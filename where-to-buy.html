How to Buy Crypto in Nigeria (Step by Step)

1

Create & Verify Account

Sign up on Binance, Breet or Yellow Card and complete KYC (required for higher limits).

2

Deposit Naira

Use Bank Transfer, OPay, PalmPay, or any supported payment method.

3

Buy Crypto via P2P or Spot

Choose seller with good rating on P2P or buy directly on spot market.

4

Withdraw to Wallet

Send your crypto to a secure wallet (Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.).

🛡️ Safety Tips for Buying Crypto in Nigeria

  • Always use platforms with good ratings and many completed trades.
  • Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) on all accounts.
  • Never share your seed phrase or private keys.
  • Start with small amounts when trying a new platform.
  • Beware of scams promising "double your money" or unrealistic rates.
  • Use official apps/websites only (check URL carefully).
